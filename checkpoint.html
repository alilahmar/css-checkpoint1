Amazighs are an ethnicity of several nations mostly indigenous to North Africa
and some northern parts of West Africa.Historically,
Berber nations spoke the Berber languages, which is a branch of the Afroasiatic language family.
There are about 25-30 million Berbers in North Africa who still speak the Berber languages.
